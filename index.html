Venzuelan Migrant Rates

By Juliet La Sala

The number of Venezuelan migrants escaping poverty, authoritarianism and economic collapse as increased in recent years. According to a CBS News report by Camilo Montoya Valdez, it is the largest refugee crisis recorded in the Western hemisphere, greater than the number of migrants leaving Syria and Ukraine. About 20% of the Venezuelan population has left since 2014. Venezuelan migrants flee economic strife
